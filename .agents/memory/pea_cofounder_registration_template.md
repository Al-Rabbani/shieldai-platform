# PEA — Co-Founder Registration Invitation Template
**Saved: 2026-06-01**
**Version: 1.0 — Official**

## Usage
This is the official template for inviting Co-Founders to register on the PEA platform.
It is sent by admin when a Founder nominates a Co-Founder.

## Subject Line
```
Co-Founder Nomination & Registration Request – Innovator Founder Visa Endorsement Process ([VENTURE NAME])
```

## Dynamic Placeholders
- `[Applicant Full Name]` → applicant_name
- `[FOUNDER/COMPANY NAME]` → founder's company (e.g., Global Digital Streams LTD)
- `[VENTURE NAME]` → venture_name (e.g., RABBANI AI ECO SYSTEM)
- `[INSERT REGISTRATION LINK]` → https://primeendorsement.com/api/functions/peaRegister?token={session_token}
- `[INSERT PLATFORM URL]` → https://primeendorsement.com
- `[INSERT SUPPORT EMAIL]` → admin@primeendorsement.com
- `[AUTO GENERATED ID]` → reference_code (e.g., PEA-2026-704377)

### Co-Founder Nomination Notice

Based on information submitted to the Prime Endorsement Authority, you have been identified as a proposed strategic contributor and Co-Founder associated with the **[VENTURE NAME]**, a high-growth innovation-driven initiative led by **[FOUNDER/COMPANY NAME]**.

In support of the proposed project structure and founder ecosystem, an application has been initiated on your behalf to begin structured assessment for endorsement consideration under the **Innovator Founder Visa Route**.

Please note that your nomination as a Co-Founder does not constitute endorsement approval and remains subject to successful registration, programme compliance, eligibility review, innovation assessment, due diligence, and endorsement evaluation.

### Registration Required – Action Needed

To proceed with your onboarding and endorsement consideration, you are required to complete your registration through the secure Prime Endorsement Authority Platform Application.

**Secure Registration Portal:**
[INSERT REGISTRATION LINK]

Kindly complete your registration carefully and ensure all required information and supporting documentation are submitted accurately to avoid delays in processing.

### How to Complete Your Registration

**Step 1 – Access the Registration Portal**
Click on the secure registration link provided and create your applicant account.

**Step 2 – Create Your Secure Applicant Profile**
Complete your account registration by providing accurate personal and professional details including:
- Full legal name (as shown on passport or identification documents)
- Date of birth, nationality and country of residence
- Residential address, telephone number and email address
- Professional background and employment history
- Educational qualifications and certifications
- Linked professional profiles (if applicable)

**Step 3 – Co-Founder & Project Participation Information**
As a nominated Co-Founder of the **[VENTURE NAME]**, you may be requested to provide:
- Proposed role within the project
- Area of expertise, contribution, or operational responsibility
- Professional experience relevant to innovation, technology, entrepreneurship, business growth, investment, operations, engineering, research, compliance, marketing, finance, or leadership
- Details of prior startup, innovation, business, or leadership experience (where applicable)

**Step 4 – Upload Supporting Documents**

**Required Documents:**
A. Personal Identification — Valid passport (bio-data page), national ID (if applicable), passport photograph
B. Academic & Professional — CV/Resume, educational certificates, professional certifications
C. Business & Innovation — Portfolio, business profile, innovation contribution statement, startup/project evidence, LinkedIn profile
D. Compliance — Proof of address, declarations, compliance acknowledgements

### What Happens After Registration?

1. **Registration Review** — Your onboarding information will undergo structured verification and eligibility review.
2. **Payment Invitation** — Where eligible, you will receive an official payment invitation through the PEA Platform.
3. **Application Activation** — Following successful payment, your endorsement application will be activated for structured review.
4. **Tracking Portal Access** — A secure applicant tracking link shall be issued to monitor your application progress.

### Important Notice
1. Nomination as a Co-Founder does not guarantee endorsement, visa, or immigration approval.
2. All applicants remain subject to programme eligibility, due diligence, innovation assessment, compliance screening, and endorsement criteria.
3. Submission of incomplete or inaccurate information may delay or affect processing outcomes.
4. Official communications shall only be issued through authorised Prime Endorsement Authority channels.
